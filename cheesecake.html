cheesecake

cheesecake is often mistaken for a gooey, disgusting, cheesy dessert that is overall just revolting. but its not. if you would like to taste revolting, please visit one of the other pages on my website that talk about desserts like banana pudding, coconut cake, and jello. now, back to cheesecake. although there is a misconception about cheesecake, its actually quite delicious. It has an amazing texture and taste: a soft but not soggy texture in the cake part, with a harder but still easy to bite bottom. the taste is very hard to explain. its sweet but not too sweet, and doesn't actually taste that much like cheese.

overall, cheesecake is a very yummy dessert to eat, and its not as hard to make as pavlova, so i would definitely recommend trying it. my rating is:

23/10
