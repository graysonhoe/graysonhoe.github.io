ratings and recipes for the worst and best possible desserts

by grayson hoe

there are many desserts, and a lot of them are delicious--only if they're made correctly. but, there are also many desserts that are absolute punishment to put in your mouth. in my opinion, here are the worst desserts (that i've tasted):

  • banana pudding
  • jello
  • coconut things

i think that those are some of the worst desserts i've ever had to put in my mouth, and although there are quite a few of them, there are also many really amazing desserts out there. here are some of what i believe to be the best desserts you could ever eat:

  • pavlova
  • cheesecake
  • profiteroles
